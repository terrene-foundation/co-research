# Structural Decision Records

Structural decision records go here. Created by `/deliberate` command.

Each record documents:

- What was decided
- Why (the rationale)
- What alternatives were considered and rejected
- Confidence level
- Reviewer impact (how this affects reception at the target venue)

Naming convention: `NNNN-[topic-slug].md` (e.g., `0001-argument-ordering.md`)

These records prevent re-debating settled questions and provide an audit trail of the paper's intellectual development.
